Q&A

Welcome to my website. I answered some questions that I've been asked to answer in a Q&A format.

Why did you decide to study Software Development?

It took me a long time to finally decide on what I was going to study. I wasn't good at anything and I didn't really like any of the options presented to me. My dean thought I would like this study because I like games, so it was related to a hobby of mine. I ended up enjoying it way more than I thought I would. That's when I decided that I wanted to continue on this path.

How are you staying motivated for school?

Well... First things first I am not a high school graduate, which means I have to finish my current study or I'll get into trouble. I also found a group of friends and that makes it more fun and easier to stay motivated for school. We help each other out. So far the teachers have been nice as well.

What are you prepared to work on in your spare time?

Of course I'm willing to work on my projects if they're not finished yet, I think that's nothing special though. I'm also currently learning how to touch type. I thought that learning this would certainly be benificial for this study.
